About Us

Mediline Medical & Diagnostic Ltd. started its journey in 2003 with the promise to reach better and modern healthcare facilities to the mass. It is approved by concerned Government authorities like Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Directorate General of Health Services, Dhaka City Corporation, Ministry of Environment and Bangladesh Atomic Energy Commission. Your sincere co-operation and auspicious support enthrusted us to become stable in today’s strong position, breaking all the limitations and hindrance were really grateful!!

Mediline Medical & Diagnostic Ltd. has already been able to achieve patients’ trust with the co-ordination of expert, enthusiastic and dedicated employers, skilled technicians, specialist doctors, latest equipments & machines, better lab services and overall serene and cordial environment. Our new management is working relentlessly to make the healthcare system more people oriented and affordable. We can proudly say that under the roof we are working as a family team.
